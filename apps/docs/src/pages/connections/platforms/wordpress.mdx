## How to connect?

<Steps>
### Step 1

Go to **My Account** -> **Settings** -> **Connections**.

### Step 2

Under **Platforms**, choose **WordPress** and click **Connect**.

### Step 3

If you are ok to share mentioned scopes, click **Connect**.

### Step 4

You will be redirected to WordPress authorize page. Choose your site and authorize Publish Studio.

</Steps>
